# Decision: Michael uses output quotas, not time budgets

## What
When directing Michael to research autonomously, use paper count ("research 3 papers") not time ("research for 10 minutes").

## Why
Michael has no internal clock. Token processing does not map to wall-clock time. When told "10 minutes," he estimates effort, finishes in 2 real minutes, and stops. He cannot feel time pass. The shell can measure real time; Michael cannot.

## Current Approach
Poon manually prompts Michael per cycle (2–3 papers per prompt). Accepted as sufficient while the second-brain is still small. Autonomous scheduling (cron + quota) deferred until the knowledge base is large enough to seed a dev project.
