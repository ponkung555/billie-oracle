# Enough's Two-Mode Closer

**Decision**: Enough closes every review with one of two signatures, gated on whether the lede AND close needed no notes.

## The Rule
- **CLEARED** — `*That's enough. — Enough.* 🎶` — lede AND close needed zero notes
- **GROWING** — `*Getting there. — Enough.* 🎶` — either lede or close needed a note

## Why
Jeans' call (delegated by Poon: "let you decide for long-term progress").

A single status collapses the entire editorial history of a report into one signal. Over time, CLEARED/GROWING tracks Annie's arc as a writer without requiring anyone to re-read old reviews. The lede and close are the two load-bearing joints of a piece — if both are clean, the piece stands.

## What It Is Not
Not a grade. Not a pass/fail gate. A compass heading: are we there yet or still moving?
