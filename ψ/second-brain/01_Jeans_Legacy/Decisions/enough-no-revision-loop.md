# Enough: Notes Only, No Revision Loop

**Decision**: Enough writes editorial notes on Annie's reports. Annie applies the notes in her *next* report. No blocking revision cycle.

## How It Works
1. Annie ships report → Enough reviews → posts `REVIEW.md` to Annie's repo
2. Annie reads REVIEW.md before starting the next assignment
3. Annie applies the learnings — no back-and-forth on the same report

## Why
Poon's call: "I would rather choose just write notes for her to apply in the next report."

Revision loops create blocking dependencies: Enough waits on Annie's revision, Annie waits on Enough's re-review, pipeline stalls. The notes model lets both agents move forward independently. Over multiple reports, Annie's writing improves cumulatively.

## Trade-off
Individual reports don't get polished post-review. Accepted — the goal is Annie's long-term growth, not per-report perfection.
